About

E.R.T.I.E., or Emergency Response Team Infiltration and Extraction, is an indoor navigation web app for rescue teams. It converts building floor plans into 3D models that visualize entry points, doorways, and windows. Emergency operators, who dispatch and manage first responders, use E.R.T.I.E. to find the shortest path to locations in a building during an emergency.
